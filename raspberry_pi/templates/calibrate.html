Remove all weight from the scale
Ensure the conveyor belt is completeley empty. The system will tare (zero) the scale when you click Start.
Place exactly 5.0kg on the scale

The calibration factor has been saved to ESP32 memory and will persist across reboots. You can now remove the calibration weight.

Thermocouple Calibration

Calibrate the sealing temperature sensor using the MLX90614 IR reference.

Preparation steps
1. Heat the sealing system to a stable tempreature (150C)
2. Wait for temperature to stabilize (abobut 5minutes)
3. Click "Calibrate" below to measure with MLX90614 IR sensor
